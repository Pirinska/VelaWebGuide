About Vela Guide

The Vela Guide is the brainchild of Iliyana Pirinska, conceived as an additional project to support her honours dissertation on "Human-centred design in digital healthcare" at Edinburgh Napier University. This innovative web-based guide responds to a clear need identified through a survey, in which participants expressed a desire for a comprehensive resource on gynaecological surgeries.

The Vela Guide bridges this gap by offering accessible, well-structured content about the surgical process, providing pragmatic checklists, and incorporating a note-taking feature to track recovery progress. Its design is deeply rooted in empathy, emphasising usability and clarity to empower users during their healthcare journey. This guide stands as a testament to the importance of user-centred design in crafting digital health tools that resonate with the needs and experiences of patients.

Please note that the content provided within the Vela Guide has not been medically verified and should not be considered as a substitute for professional medical advice. Currently, the guide serves as an educational resource and is expected to undergo further development. Future updates will focus on incorporating medically reviewed information to enhance its reliability and comprehensiveness.
